Taste of April - Qing Dumpling

We invited 12 hallmates to participate in making Qing Dumplings. During the activity, we first briefly introduced the history of Qingming Festival. Then we let all the participants to experience the whole process of making Qing dumplings with the ingredients we prepared. We also talked about the story about why we eat Qing dumplings on Qingming Festival. We hope that it can promote the communication among our hall mates and enhance their friendship.
