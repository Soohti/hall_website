A Loving, Caring and Inspiring Home for City Learners

Our Mission is to nurture a Community of learners, who are:
    Excellent in academic and social endeavors.
    Global in understanding and experience.
    Compassionate in heart.
    Collaborative in action.


Hall Nine Core Values:

Wisdom - Making the right choices in life.
Commitment- Being committed to others and to ourselves.
Respect- Respecting the tradition for a better future.
Compassion- Being kind to all creatures.
Love- Being passionate with life.
Etiquette- Exhibiting exemplary, cultured behavior.
Justice- Standing up for justice with personal integrity.
Harmony- Including and embracing others.
Balance- Maintaining a balanced life.
