Transportation around our hall:

Conveniently situated at the interchange of two railway lines, City University Student Residence is easily accessible by various forms of public transport.

By Mass Transit Railway (MTR)
Kwun Tong Line / East Rail Line: If you are coming by MTR Kwun Tong Line, get off at Kowloon Tong station. Follow the signs to City University (Exit C), then take Exit C2, which leads to the Festival Walk shopping centre. Go up to Level LG1 and find the escalator next to Shop LG1-10, which will take you to one end of the CityU underpass. Follow the signage in front of the underpass and walk along Tat Chee Avenue, the Student Residence is uphill (8-minute walk) at the end of the road. The website of MTR provides you with the latest information about routes and fares: http://www.mtr.com.hk.

By Taxi
Ask the taxi driver to take you to Student Residence of City University of Hong Kong, 22 Cornwall Street (intersection of Cornwall Street and Tat Chee Avenue), Kowloon Tong 九龍塘歌和老街22香港城市大學學生宿舍(歌和老街和達之路交界). When you get out of the taxi, you are in front of the Student Residence Office.

By Bus
There are Buses 2B, 2F, 86C, 86P, 86X, E22 and Mini-buses 72, 73, 85 just near by the Student Residence. They can go around most of the place in Kowloon.

From Airport
If you land in Hong Kong by air, the following means of transportation can take you from Hong Kong International Airport at Chek Lap Kok to CityU.

  • Airport Express Line and Mass Transit Railway (MTR): You can ride on the Airport Express Line from the Airport Station to Tsing Yi Station, and then change to MTR from Tsing Yi Station to Kowloon Tong Station.
  • Bus: Citybus bus number E22, running from Chek Lap Kok to Lam Tin (North). Get off at Shek Kip Mei Park, Cornwall Street. The Student Residence at Cornwall Street is just beside the bus stop. The website of Citybus provides you with the latest information about bus route and fare: http:// www.citybus.com.hk. 
  • Taxi: You can take a taxi at the Airport to Student Residence of City University of Hong Kong, 22 Cornwall Street (intersection of Cornwall Street and Tat Chee Avenue), Kowloon Tong 九龍塘歌和老街22號 香港城市大學學生宿舍(歌和老街和達之路交界). Extra cost is charged on each piece of luggage. The website of Hong Kong International Airport provides you approximate taxi fare information: http://www.hongkongairport.com/eng/transport/to-from-airport/taxi.html.

    Eating:

    Homey Kitchen inside the Student Residence is the most convenient restaurant to the residents.
    There are also many restaurants in the CityU campus and in Festival Walk.
    Some Hong Kong style restaurants can be found in Nam Shan Estate (around 15 mins by walking).
    You can get information of nearly all restaurants in Hong Kong from Open Rice.

    Shopping:

    The nearest supermarket is "Taste" in Festival Walk. Most of the daily necessities can be bought there.
    There is a traditional market in Nam Shan Estate where prices are cheaper.
    If you want to find good quality goods and food at low price, you should go to Sham Shui Po (It's a good place to discover great restaurants as well).

    Air-conditioner:

    The air-conditioner will cause you $1 for 70 minutes when it is turned in maximum cold.
    But it only causes around $4 for whole night when it is in middle cool level.
    The room key-card is used to pay the air-con. The card can be charged with a machine in front of Hall 5.
