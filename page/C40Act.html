Professor Edmund Ko Cup 2014-2015

 Aquatic (2014)

We have some good news to share with all of you. This year both our Women’s and Men’s swimmers did us proud in the Professor Edmond Ko (PEK) Cup Aquatic Competition 2014. For the Women’s 4x50m Freestyle Relay, I am very proud to announce that we came in 2nd in the competition with the timing of 2’28.24”. For the Men’s 4x50m Freestyle Relay, we also did very well, coming in 4th with the timing of 2’00.75”. The winning of 2nd placing for the Women’s 4x50m Freestyle relay marks a new milestone in the history of Hall 9’s participation in the PEK Aquatic competition– because this is our first PEK Aquatic Competition medal!!!!! YAYYYYYY We’d like to offer our heartfelt congratulations to both the Women’s and Men’s team, and also thank them for their remarkable effort in helping to bring glory and pride to our hall. We’d like also to thank the supporters who came down to support the team. Stay tuned to our Facebook page as we'd be uploading the photos taken during the PEK Aquatic Competition really soon.
