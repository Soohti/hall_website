Hall 9 Scholarships for Overall Excellence

Sir Gordon and Lady Ivy Wu Hall (Hall 9) is committed to nurturing and developing its residents into sensible, considerate, intellectual, loving and caring members of the community by providing them with a warm, caring and civilised environment with a strong emphasis on the need to comply with Hall and University Student Residence rules and regulations. The scholarships will be awarded to acknowledge those residents who are able to display Sir Gordon and Lady Ivy Wu Hall's nature and spirit as well as to achieve scholastic excellence.
