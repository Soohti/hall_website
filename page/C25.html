Provisional Hall Residents’ Association

Provisional Hall Residents’ Association is the independent student body and the driving force behind most of the social programs for the halls. They offer a forum for you to voice concerns and propose new ideas. Floor committees and floor representatives are of a more informal nature. They aim to create and cultivate a supportive network among residents living on the same floor.
