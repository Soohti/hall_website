Inter-hall Contest Fun Day

The ICFD was set up to promote inter-hall interaction, and exchange between local and non-local residents. Students enjoyed as much as they learnt in organising the events and participating in the competitions*.

 Tug of War (2023-24)

A new academic year brought with it a new and exciting ICFD Event – Tug of War. Congratulations to our hall for securing 3rd place in the Tug of War competition! Both the boys' and girls' teams gave it their all, demonstrating incredible effort and outstanding sportsmanship. We're proud of their hard work and determination. Well done, teams!

 Badminton (2023-2024)

Despite not securing the top spot, our hall showcased remarkable talent and determination in the Badminton competition. Each of our teams delivered impressive performances, coming close to clinching prize positions. Their dedication and skill were evident throughout the tournament, reflecting positively on our hall's spirit and sportsmanship. Congratulations to all participants for their outstanding efforts!

 Basketball (2023-2024)

In the Basketball ICFD, both our boys' and girls' teams displayed remarkable skill and teamwork. Despite their outstanding performance, luck wasn't on our side this time. Nevertheless, their dedication and hard work were evident on the court, and they represented our hall with pride and sportsmanship. Let's continue to support our teams as they strive for success in future competitions!

 Singing Contest (2023-2024)

PEK Inter-hall Singing Contest is an annual festival for all residents. It has now become a tradition for CityU Residence. It is no doubt that Singing Contest will be considered as one of the most precious memories in university life for all. Looking forward to the outstanding performances next year!
